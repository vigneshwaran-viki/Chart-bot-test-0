Schedule appointments

All business types, ranging from healthcare organization to home maintenance providers, can benefit from leveraging chatbots to schedule and modify customers’ appointments.

Chatbots are linked to the organization’s calendar to provide the customer with information about available staff and time slots, as well as allow them to book appointments without

needing to contact front desks.


Book tickets

Chatbots can guide the customer through the process of purchasing tickets for activities and events such as conferences, concerts, shows, tours, etc.

For example, in 2017, Shell, the multinational oil and gas company, used a Facebook Messenger chatbot to sell tickets to their annual Shell Eco Marathon

where engineers and students compete to build an energy-efficient vehicle. The event was attended by 25,000 visitors.
